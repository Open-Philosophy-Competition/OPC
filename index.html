The Competition

The Open Philosophy Competition (OPC) is a free nation-wide philosophy competition for high school students. Our judges will score each submission for its originality, thoughtfulness, and integration of philosophy with modern-day issues. The top submissions will be eligible for awards.

Contestants must write an essay or create a video in response to their chosen prompt. Please address one of the prompts below.

Tentative 2022 Prompts
  1. Do we have free will? Is the universe inherently deterministic, or are we free agents with choice?

  2. How would you define the opposite of crime? Be creative.

  3. Intent vs impact — which one should be prioritized when determining legal or social consequences?

About Us

We're a group of enthusiastic high school students who saw a lack of pre-collegiate philosophical education in the US (and frankly, the world). Through our competition series and community, we hope to spark a love for philosophy and a passion for truth.
